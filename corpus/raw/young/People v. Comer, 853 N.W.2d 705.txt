Opinion




 Order
On order of the Court, the application for leave to appeal the January 27, 2014 order of the Court of Appeals is considered. We DIRECT the St. Clair County Prosecuting Attorney to answer the application for leave to appeal within 28 days after the date of this order.
The application for leave to appeal remains pending.
Concur by: YOUNG  
Concur




Young, C.J. (concurring).
I concur in the decision to direct the prosecutor to respond to the application for leave to appeal and would further ask the prosecutor to address whether there is a temporal limit on the circuit court's ability to correct an invalid judgment of sentence. See MCR 6.429(A).
