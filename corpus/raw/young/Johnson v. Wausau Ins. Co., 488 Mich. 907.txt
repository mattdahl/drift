Opinion




  [*853]  Order
On order of the Court, the application for leave to appeal the March 24, 2009 judgment of the Court of Appeals is considered and, pursuant to MCR 7.302(H)(1), in lieu of granting leave to appeal, we VACATE the judgment of the Court of Appeals and the October 11, 2007 order of the Genesee Circuit Court and we REMAND this case to the Genesee Circuit Court for reconsideration in light of Regents of the Univ. of Mich. v. Titan Ins. Co., 487 Mich 289, 791 N.W.2d 897, 2010 Mich. LEXIS 1658 (2010) (Docket No. 136905). The motion for miscellaneous relief is GRANTED.
Concur by: Maura D. Corrigan; Robert P. Young, Jr.  
Concur
